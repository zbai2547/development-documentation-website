Design brief

Design a web page for the "BRIGHTER FUTURE CAT RESCUE" charity organization, which can support and promote their cause online. The website will focus on functions such as volunteer service, donation, merchandise and pet adoption.

Resaearch method

I will use a research method-questionnaire after mockups display

This time the questionnaire will contain 4 questions, and 10 respondents will answer the questions.

Eighty percent of people chose "yes" in this question. After asking their opinions, I will continue to use the navigation bar in mockups. At the same time, I will continue to use the same page allocation as in mockups.

Most respondents chose "yes" in this question, and after asking their opinions, I will continue to use the colors in mockups. However, based on the opinions of some respondents, I will uniformly use the font "Times New Roman" to ensure the clarity of the text.

Most respondents chose "no" in this question. After asking their opinions, I found that they thought I had some useless pages, such as the about us page. At the same time, according to the requirements of the design brief, I decided to integrate the about us page and the homepage.

Most of the respondents chose "yes" in this question. After asking their opinions, I found that they thought my yellow donate button was very attractive and very special in the whole page. I will continue to use this style.

Target Market

The target market for “Brighter Future Cat Rescue” website will be:

  • User who likes animals
  • User who likely to adopt animals
  • User who interesting in donating to animals
  • User who interesting in participating in volunteer activities about animals
  • User who interested in buying merchandise from charity organizations

Fonts

In the selection of fonts, I will only use one font-Times New Roman. This font is used as the font of the paper, I think it can express the meaning of the text more clearly. At the same time, using only one font can ensure the consistency of the text on the web page.
